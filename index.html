About

I am a PhD scholar in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at IIT Indore. My research develops scalable algorithms and systems for modern networks, especially sketch-based traffic measurement, heavy-hitter detection, packet scheduling, programmable data planes, and privacy-preserving communication. I aim to convert research ideas into practical, efficient, and deployable network solutions.

Profile

I work on network monitoring problems end to end — from algorithm design to practical implementation on programmable data planes. I enjoy translating research ideas into systems that actually run.

Interests

Network monitoring, sketch-based data structures, packet scheduling, traffic analysis, machine learning for the data plane, and privacy-preserving communication.
