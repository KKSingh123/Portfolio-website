Kunvar Kanhaiya

I am currently working on advanced research in network monitoring using sketch-based data structures, focusing on scalable and memory-efficient traffic measurement techniques. My work also explores packet scheduling algorithms to improve network performance, fairness, and latency in high-speed systems. In addition, I am interested in privacy-preserving communications, where I apply concepts from AI and Machine Learning to protect user data and traffic patterns from inference attacks.

About Me

I am a PhD scholar in the CSE Department at IIT Indore, working in computer network monitoring. My research focuses on network monitoring using sketch-based data structures, packet scheduling, and privacy-preserving communications using AI/ML. I aim to design scalable, memory-efficient, and practical networking solutions for real-world systems.
